自我

Ego
赛斯在《灵魂永生》《个人实相的本质》《未知的实相》《群体事件的本质》《心灵的本质》《健康之道》《早期课》第1-9册中阐述
我把这看似无意识的部分称为内我，因为它指导内在活动。它整合那些不是通过身体感官感知的信息。它是存在于三维世界之外的那个实相的内在感知者。它携带了你每一世存在的记忆。
"I call this seemingly unconscious the 'inner ego,' for it directs inner activities. It correlates information that is perceived not through the physical senses, but through other inner channels. It is the inner perceiver of reality that exists beyond the three-dimensional. It carries within it the memory of each of your past existences."
Seth Speaks, Session 512
外我与内我协同运作：一个让你在已知世界中运作，另一个为你带来那些精细的内在感知——没有这些感知，物质存在无法维持。
"The 'outer ego' and the inner ego operate together, the one to enable you to manipulate in the world that you know, the other to bring you those delicate inner perceptions without which physical existence could not be maintained."
Seth Speaks, Session 512
还有一部分你——那个同时形成了内我和外我的更深层身份——决定了你要在这个地方、这个时间成为一个物质存在。这是你身份的核心。
"There is however a portion of you, the deeper identity who forms both the inner ego and the outer ego, who decided that you would be a physical being in this place and in this time. This is the core of your identity."
Seth Speaks, Session 512
你的身份感和连续感并不依赖于自我。
"Your sense of identity and continuity is not dependent upon the ego."
Seth Speaks, Session 512
内我是星光体的我。它紧密联系着存有(entity)，连接纯心灵实相与物质实相。
"The inner ego is then the 'I' of the astral body. It is closely allied with the entity, and connects a reality that is purely psychic with a reality that is mainly physical."
Early Sessions, Book 5, Session 216
作为一个物种，你们变得自我束缚(ego bound)了——在精神上僵硬，直觉的部分被否认或扭曲到无法辨认。
"As a species you have grown 'ego bound' instead, held in a spiritual rigidity, with the intuitive portions of the self either denied or distorted beyond any recognition."
Seth Speaks, Session 511
自我是一个嫉妒的神，它希望自己的利益得到满足。它不愿承认任何自己感觉不自在且无法理解的维度。它本应成为助力，却被允许变成暴君。即便如此，它比人们通常认为的要坚韧得多、好学得多。它并不像看起来那样天生僵化。它的好奇心可以产生巨大价值。
"The ego is a jealous god, and it wants its interests served. It does not want to admit the reality of any dimensions except those within which it feels comfortable and can understand. It was meant to be an aid but it has been allowed to become a tyrant. Even so, it is much more resilient and eager to learn than is generally supposed. It is not natively as rigid as it seems. Its curiosity can be of great value."
Seth Speaks, Session 512
自我常常像一道大坝，拦住其他感知——不是因为它天生如此，而是因为你被教导说自我的作用是限制性的而非扩展性的。你实际上认为自我是自我中很弱的部分，必须防御其他更强大、更具说服力、更危险的部分。你给它戴上眼罩，违背了它的本性。自我确实想要理解和诠释物质实相，想要在其中生存。但你给它戴上眼罩后，又说它看不见。
"Often the ego acts as a dam, to hold back other perceptions — not because it was meant to, but simply because you have been taught that the purpose of an ego is restrictive rather than expanding. You actually imagine that the ego is a very weak portion of the self, that it must defend itself against other areas of the self that are far stronger. You put blinders upon it, and then say that it cannot see."
Seth Speaks, Session 532
现在的你，和五年前已经不是同一个自我了，但你没有察觉到这个变化。
"You do not have the same ego now that you had five years ago, but you are not aware of the change."
The Nature of Personal Reality, Session 615
自我从心灵的结构中升起，如同眼睛从身体的结构中升起。它看不见自己，正如眼睛看不见自己。两者都向外看。
"The ego rises out of the structure of the psyche. It cannot see itself, as the eye cannot. Both look outward."
The Nature of Personal Reality, Session 615
自我无法如此改变它的焦点，但我们所说的这个内我才是做梦的那个自己——是当自我之我入睡时仍在体验的那个我。
"The ego cannot so change its focus, but the inner ego of which we have spoken is indeed the self that dreams, and is the 'I' who experiences when the ego 'I' sleeps."
Early Sessions, Book 3, Session 90
潜意识的各个阶段代表了一个个体曾经的意识自我的残余——如同活着的考古遗产。这些过去的自我与始终代表真实个体的内我重新结合。
"These various stages of the subconscious represent what was once the conscious ego of an individual, left now like living archaeological heritage. These former egos reunite with the inner ego, which always represents the true individual."
Early Sessions, Book 3, Session 90
早期希伯来神成了人类释放的自我的象征。神的行为就像一个被激怒的孩子，如果他拥有那些力量，就会发出雷电和火焰攻击敌人。人类正在浮现的自我因而带来了情感和心理的问题与挑战。
"The early Hebrew god became a symbol of man's unleashed ego. God behaved exactly as an enraged child would, had he those powers, sending thunder and lightning and fire against his enemies. Man's emerging ego therefore brought forth emotional and psychological problems and challenges."
Seth Speaks, Session 558
神因此成为一个向外投射的观念，独立于个体，与自然分离。他成为人类浮现的自我的反映，具有其全部的光辉、野蛮、力量和主宰意图。……自我从内在诞生，因此它总是在夸耀其独立的同时，又保持着对其内在起源的挥之不去的确定。
"God became an idea projected outward, independent of the individual, divorced from nature. He became the reflection of man's emerging ego, with all of its brilliance, savagery, power, and intent for mastery. The ego, having its birth from within, therefore must always boast of its independence while maintaining the nagging certainty of its inner origin."
Seth Speaks, Session 558
自我害怕失去自己的位置，害怕自己会溶解回它所来自的内在自我。但它的出现为内在自我提供了一种新的反馈——一种不同的视角，通过这种视角，内在自我得以窥见以前未曾意识到的发展可能。
"The ego feared for its position, frightened that it would dissolve back into the inner self from which it came. Yet in its emergence it provided the inner self with a new kind of feedback, a different view; through this, the inner self was able to glimpse possibilities of development of which it had not previously been aware."
Seth Speaks, Session 558
你们许多复兴的神秘学派鼓吹消灭欲望、消灭自我以便将物质元素转化为更高层次。在所有这类情况下，个体清晰的精神和生物完整性都受到损害，你当下时刻的宝贵直接性在很大程度上丢失了。
"Many of your resurrected occult schools speak of a recommended death of desire, the annihilation of the ego, for the transmutation of physical elements to finer levels. In all such cases the clear spiritual and biological integrity of the individual suffers, and the precious immediacy of your moments is largely lost."
The Individual and the Nature of Mass Events, Session 805
就像你有完全有意识、面向物质世界的自我一样，你也有我称之为内我的东西，它面向内在实相。内我是完全有意识的。
"As you have an ego, fully conscious, directed toward the physical world, you also have what I call an inner ego, directed toward inner reality. The inner ego is fully conscious."
The Individual and the Nature of Mass Events, Session 825
你通常把自己的意识心当作你的自我。它面向物质生活中的行动。许多学派似乎有一种奇怪的想法，认为自我比其他部分低级或自私，并想象它肯定比内在自我或灵魂更低等。但实际上，将自我的各部分分开是不可能的。
"You usually think of your conscious mind as your ego. It is directed toward action in physical life. Many schools of thought seem to have the curious idea that the ego is inferior to other portions of the self, or 'selfish,' and imagine it to be definitely of a lower quality than the inner self, or the soul. In the first place, it is really impossible to separate portions of the self."
The Way Toward Health, Session 1
大多数人不知道他们确实可以接触到这种内在觉知。这个内我不应被理解为优于你的普通心智——它不应被视为与你的普通心智分离的东西。你的自我和普通意识将你所有的物质经验汇聚到焦点，使物质经验的辉煌精确成为可能。
"Most people do not realize that they can indeed have access to this inner awareness. This inner ego or inner self should not be thought of as superior to your ordinary mind. It should not be thought of as something separate from your ordinary mind. Your ego and your ordinary consciousness bring into focus all of your physical experiences, and make possible the brilliant preciseness of physical experience."
The Way Toward Health, Session 1
内我始终认同其源头身份——作为宇宙中被珍爱的、个体化的部分。它知道作为其遗产的宇宙之爱。通过意识到这些事实，外在自我可以开始感受到更快速的支持和滋养。这种知识可以让它放松、放手，感受到自己的生命被包裹在安全之中。
"The inner ego always identifies with its source-identity as a beloved, individualized portion of the universe. It is aware of the universal love that is its heritage. Through being made aware of these facts, the exterior ego can begin to feel a quicker sense of support and nourishment."
The Way Toward Health, Session 1
你没有在听自己内在的声音，你在听你的自我说的话。你建立了如此坚固的防御，以至于听不到内在自我的声音。你让自我变成了一个伪造的自我。
"You are not listening to what your own inner voice says. You are listening to what your ego says. You have built defenses so strongly that you have not heard the voice of the inner self. You have allowed the ego to become a counterfeit self."
Early Sessions, Book 8, Session 342
各种自我结构同时存在。它们完全不受时间框架的影响。例如，在轮回中，一个经历过美国内战生活的自我，现在知道另一个可能经历过公元3000年生活的自我。
"The various ego structures therefore exist simultaneously. They are entirely outside of any time framework. Even in reincarnation, an ego who experienced a Civil War life is now aware of another ego who may have experienced life in the year three thousand."
Early Sessions, Book 9, Session 460
想象一个身体，具有完全运作的身体意识，没有疾病或缺陷，但没有覆盖一切的自我导向意识——一个在没有自我持续指挥的情况下运作的身体。
"Imagine a body with a fully operating body consciousness, not diseased or defective, but without the overriding ego-directed consciousness — a body that operates without the constant command of the ego."
Dreams, Evolution and Value Fulfillment, Vol. 1, Session 882
你是一个自己，但为了操作方便，我们可以说你有三个部分：内在自我或内我、身体意识，以及你所知道的外在自我——那个向外看的自己。
"You are one self, but for operating purposes we will say that you have three parts: the inner self or inner ego, the body consciousness, and the exterior ego that you know — the self that looks outward."
Dreams, Evolution and Value Fulfillment, Vol. 1, Session 893
